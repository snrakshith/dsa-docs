description: "commands associated with whims-usermanual portal"
---

### Solution

```javascript
const sumDigits = (number) =>
  number
    .toString()
    .split("")
    .reduce((sum, digit) => sum + Number(digit), 0);
console.log(sumDigits(1234)); // Output: 10
```
